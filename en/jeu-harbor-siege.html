Harbor Siege

Story

Dive into the heart of an old abandoned harbor, a true maze of narrow alleys, forgotten warehouses and cargo crates. In this environment steeped in history, every corner can hide an opponent.

Form your team and set out to conquer the harbor's strategic zones. The more territory you control, the more points you rack up. But beware: your opponents will do everything to take back control and turn the tide of battle.

Coordination, speed and tactical thinking will be the keys to victory in this immersive experience where every decision counts.

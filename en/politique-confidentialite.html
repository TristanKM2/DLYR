Privacy policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

D'LYR attaches great importance to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you, in full transparency, about how we collect, use, store and protect your data when you use our website www.dlyr-vr.com (hereinafter “the Site”) and our related services.

By using our Site and making bookings, you accept the practices described in this policy.

1. Data controller

The controller of the personal data collected on the Site is:

  • D'LYR S.A.S., with share capital of €100,000.00
  • Registered office: 1-3, boulevard Charles de Gaulle, 92700 Colombes, France
  • Email address: contact@dlyr-vr.com

2. What data do we collect?

We only collect the data strictly necessary to provide you with our virtual reality arena and food services. This data includes:

  • Booking and purchase data : surname, first name, email address, phone number, history of your game sessions and orders.
  • Payment data : bank card transactions are handled in a fully encrypted and secure way by our certified payment provider. D'LYR does not collect or store any bank details on its servers.
  • Contact data : information provided when you contact us by email or via a contact form (name, email, subject of the request, message).
  • Browsing data (cookies) : IP address, browser type, pages visited and browsing time (see article 7 on cookies).

3. Why do we collect your data? (purposes)

The processing of your personal data rests on clear legal bases (performance of a contract, legal obligation or your consent). We use your data to:

  • Manage your bookings and customer accounts : process orders, issue tickets, send email confirmations and handle session changes/rescheduling.
  • Provide customer service : answer your questions, information or quote requests (in particular for corporate events, birthdays, stag/hen parties).
  • Communication and marketing : if you have expressly agreed (by ticking the corresponding box), we may send you information about our new games, special offers and D'LYR news. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Security and compliance : prevent computer fraud and comply with our accounting and legal obligations.

4. Data retention period

D'LYR undertakes not to keep your personal data beyond the period strictly necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Customer data (accounts and bookings) : kept for the duration of the commercial relationship, then archived for 3 years after your last activity (last purchase or last contact) for marketing purposes.
  • Billing and accounting data : kept for 10 years, in accordance with the legal obligations of the French Commercial Code.
  • Prospect data (contact requests without purchase) : deleted or anonymised at most 3 years after your last contact.

5. Who has access to your data? (recipients)

Your personal data is intended exclusively for D'LYR and its authorised staff for the management of the site and the venue.

We never sell, rent or transfer your personal data to third parties for commercial purposes. It may however be passed on to our technical subcontractors and trusted partners, solely to ensure our services run smoothly:

  • Our secure online payment provider.
  • Our web host.
  • Our booking management and emailing tool (ticketing).

These providers have limited access to your data and are contractually bound to protect it in accordance with GDPR regulations.

6. Security of your data

We implement rigorous physical, technical and organisational security measures to protect your data against any unauthorised access, modification, disclosure or destruction.

7. Cookie management

The Site uses “cookies” to improve your browsing experience, analyse site traffic and secure the booking process.

  • Strictly necessary cookies : essential for browsing the site and completing your online booking. They cannot be disabled.
  • Audience measurement cookies : help us understand how visitors browse our site so we can improve its usability.

On your first visit to the Site, a cookie management banner appears. You are free to accept or refuse all or some of the cookies subject to consent, and to change your choices at any time by clicking the cookie management link at the bottom of the page.

8. Your rights (under the GDPR)

As a user, you have full rights over the personal data concerning you:

  • Right of access : obtain confirmation that data concerning you is being processed and receive a copy of it.
  • Right to rectification : request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”) : request the deletion of your personal data under certain legal conditions.
  • Right to object : object at any time to the processing of your data, in particular for commercial prospecting purposes (e.g. newsletter).
  • Right to portability : retrieve the data you have provided to us in a structured, readable format.

To exercise your rights, simply send your signed request by email to the following address: contact@dlyr-vr.com, specifying the subject of your request.

If, after contacting us, you believe your rights are not being respected, you may lodge a complaint with the CNIL, the French data protection authority (at cnil.fr).

9. Changes to the privacy policy

D'LYR reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy at any time, in particular to comply with legal, regulatory or technological developments. We invite you to check this page regularly.

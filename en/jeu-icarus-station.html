Icarus Station

Story

An unprecedented disaster strikes the Icarus-7 space station. As it pursues its mission at the far reaches of space, a mysterious computer virus takes control of the station's critical systems. Within minutes, communications are cut, security protocols disabled and the ship's commander neutralised. You are among the last survivors.

With no one at the controls, the station is slowly falling apart. Airlocks are sealing shut, life-support systems are failing and entire sectors are now inaccessible. In the icy silence of space, any mistake can be fatal.

Your mission is simple: make your way through the damaged zones, reactivate the essential systems, fix the failures caused by the attack and reach the mobile extraction platform before it's too late.

But you are not alone. An unknown presence seems to be moving through the station's corridors, while the countdown to destruction draws inexorably to its end. Time is running out. The enemy is closing in. Space forgives no mistake.

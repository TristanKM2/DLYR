Snow Village

Story

Hidden deep in the snowy mountains lies Snow Village, Santa's famous village, where elves, reindeer and villagers live in harmony. But this year, the festivities are under threat.

A powerful evil sorcerer has launched an attack on the village. Ice creatures, enchanted golems and monsters from the frozen lands are invading the snow-covered streets, destroying everything in their path. The villagers are in danger and the magic of Christmas may vanish forever.

As a team, players must travel across the village, defend the houses, protect the elves and push back ever more powerful waves of enemies. As they progress, they will face the sorcerer's terrible creatures before taking on his most fearsome champions.

The fate of the village now rests in their hands.
